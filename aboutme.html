About Me

Hello! My name is Jiaying Wei. I hail from many places including China, Japan, New York, and Kansas. I currently reside in Boston where I'm pursuing a bachelor's degree in engineering at Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering.

One of the first things most people ask me when I tell them I go to an engineering school is: "what are you majoring in?" I wish I could give them a straight forward answer but the thing is, I like everything. I love spending time in the machine shop, I like typing out code for various pet projects, I want to learn more about electrical systems and why they work. A simple answer for all of this is: "I'm undecided." But I am so much more than just undecided, the world is my oyster.
