Leveraging design to help street performers recover lost photos & videos.

There are thousands of photos & videos taken of buskers (also known as street performers) every single day, but most of those files stay on the audience’s phone and never make it back to the busker themselves. The reality is that it isn’t easy to share photos with someone you’re not connected to on social media, and there’s no real incentive for people to do so either.

Whozzat creates an incentive for users to share those photos by allowing them to easily find out who’s performing just by snapping a picture. Then can give a digital tip and/or follow the busker on Whozzat to see future shows they are doing. The busker now has a collection of photos & videos to use in social media or personal use, as well as an opportunity to grow an audience that stays around longer than a single performance.

Features

  • → Snap a picture/take a video to find out who’s performing
  • → Share that photo with the busker, social media, or save it to your phone
  • → No bills, no problem - tip the busker digitally
  • → Follow buskers through the app to be notified about future performances
  • → Use the discover page to find performances to check out, and buskers to follow
