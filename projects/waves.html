Leveraging design to help busy people surf more

Surfing is a fun sport that brings us closer to nature, but because of that we are also subject to her whims. Some days there are lots of waves, and some days the ocean is completely flat. There are lots of variables that can go into a surfer’s decision on whether to go surfing or not.

There are existing apps that provide detailed meteorological data on wave height, tide graphs, sunrise & sunset times which are great for people who like the nitty gritty details. However I wanted to create something that would encourage people who are intimidated by the technical aspects of surfing to just go out and surf more. To do that, I wanted to make the decision to surf as simple as possible: Do I want to go surfing at this time? Yes or No.

Features

  • → Simple questionnaire figures out which types of waves you like and when you have time to surf
  • → Provide recommendations for locations that offer the best ride on days that you are free
  • → Each card gives suggestions for how to make the best of that particular surf session
  • → Swipe a card to the left to see alternative options at other locations
  • → Special colored cards highlight when wave conditions are atypical


Future Expansions

  • → Detailed view: For the more data loving surfer, tap the card to learn more details such as crowds, wind speed & direction, tide data, and more
  • → Calendar view: For people with more flexible schedules or want to optimize for certain conditions, see wave forecast in calendar view
  • → More water sports: Expand the app to include windsurfing, paddle boarding, sailing, and more
